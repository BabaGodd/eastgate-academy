Who We Are

Welcome to Eastgate Academy

Located in the heart of Dawenya, Tema, Eastgate Academy has been providing exceptional primary education for over a decade. Our holistic approach to learning ensures each child develops academically, socially, and emotionally.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum covering English, Mathematics, General Science, ICT, Physical Education, and Creative Arts, delivered by our team of dedicated professionals.

What We Offer

Our Academic Programs

A well-rounded curriculum designed to develop every child's full potential.

English Literature

Developing strong reading, writing, and communication skills through our comprehensive literacy program.

Mathematics

Building numerical fluency and problem-solving skills with hands-on, practical math activities.

Integrated Science

Exploring the natural world through experiments, observations, and discovery-based learning.

ICT

Developing digital literacy and computer skills to prepare students for the modern world.

Physical Education

Promoting health, fitness, and teamwork through sports and physical activities.

Creative Arts

Encouraging self-expression through visual arts, music, dance, and drama.
