⚠️ Official Payment Security Notice

Eastgate Academy will NEVER request school fees via WhatsApp, phone call or text message. All payments must be made exclusively through our official parent portal or at the school accounts office. If anyone contacts you requesting payment outside these channels, call us immediately on 0244 512 123.

Questions?

Fees FAQ

Answers to common questions about school fees and payments.

School fees are due at the beginning of each term, within the first two weeks of resumption. Late payment may attract a penalty fee of GHS 50.00 per week after the deadline.

Yes, we offer installment payment plans for families who need flexibility. Please visit the school accounts office or contact us to arrange an installment plan before the term begins.

Students with outstanding fees may be asked to stay home until payment is made. We always try to work with families first — please contact the accounts office if you are experiencing difficulties.

Tuition fees are partially refundable if a student withdraws within the first two weeks of term. After two weeks, fees are non-refundable. Registration fees are non-refundable.

Payments made through the parent portal automatically generate a digital receipt with a unique reference number. For payments made at the school office, a printed receipt is issued immediately.
