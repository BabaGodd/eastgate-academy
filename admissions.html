Enrollment Process

How to Apply.

We're Delighted You're Considering Eastgate Academy. Follow These Simple Steps to Begin.

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office to request information or schedule a school tour.

2

Application

Complete and submit the application form along with all required documents.

3

Assessment

Your child will participate in a brief age-appropriate assessment.

4

Interview

Parents meet with the headteacher to discuss expectations and your child's needs.

5

Acceptance

Receive your acceptance notification and full enrollment package.

6

Registration

Complete registration by submitting all forms and paying the required fees.

What You'll Need

Admissions Requirements.

Please Ensure You Have The Following Documents Ready Before Applying.

Creche & Kindergarten

  • Child must be at least 4 years old
  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • 2 passport-sized photographs

Basic 1 – Basic 3

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Previous school report cards
  • 2 passport-sized photographs
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)

Basic 4 – JHS

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Report cards from previous 2 years
  • 2 passport-sized photographs
  • Transfer certificate
  • Entrance assessment in English & Math
Kindergarten

Key Focus Areas

  • Phonics and early reading skills
  • Number recognition and basic math
  • Fine and gross motor skill development
  • Social skills and emotional intelligence
  • Creative expression through art and music

What We Teach

Key Subjects.

A Comprehensive Curriculum Designed To Develop Well-Rounded, Capable Learners.

English Language

Reading comprehension, writing, grammar and spelling skills.

Mathematics

Number operations, measurement, geometry and problem-solving.

Science

Scientific concepts, environmental awareness and experiments.

Social Studies

Community, culture, history and basic geography of Ghana.

Computing

Computer basics, typing skills and introduction to coding.

Creative Arts

Music, dance, drama and visual arts for creative development.
