Our Teaching Philosophy

Great Teachers Make the Difference

At Eastgate Academy, we believe that great teachers are the foundation of an exceptional education. Our staff members are carefully selected for their qualifications, experience, and most importantly, their passion for teaching and nurturing young minds.

All our teachers hold relevant degrees and teaching certifications, and participate in ongoing professional development to stay current with the latest educational methodologies.
