Eastgate Academy is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of all students, parents and staff. This policy explains how we handle your personal information on our website and portal.

1. Introduction

These Terms of Use and Privacy Policy govern your use of the Eastgate Academy website and parent portal located at eastgateacademy.netlify.app and any related services operated by Eastgate Academy, Dawenya, Tema, Ghana.

By accessing or using our website and portal you agree to be bound by these terms. If you do not agree please do not use our services.

This website and portal is operated by Eastgate Academy in partnership with KyKal Technologies who built and maintains the system.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our portal and website:

  • Student full name, class and student ID number
  • Parent or guardian full name and phone number
  • Teacher full name, staff ID and email address
  • Academic results, attendance records and assignment data
  • Fee payment history and transaction references
  • Login activity and session data
  • Browser type and device information for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide access to the parent and teacher portal
  • To display academic results, attendance and assignments
  • To process and record school fee payments
  • To send school announcements and important notices
  • To maintain school records and generate report cards
  • To improve the security and performance of the portal

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Data Protection

All data is stored securely using Supabase — a trusted, enterprise-grade database platform with bank-level encryption. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/SSL
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Role-based access control — parents can only see their own child's data
  • Automatic session timeout after 10 minutes of inactivity
  • Row Level Security on all database tables

5. Children's Privacy

Eastgate Academy takes the privacy of children extremely seriously. Our portal is designed for use by parents and teachers only — not by students directly.

  • Student data is only accessible to the student's own parent or guardian
  • No student under 18 is required to create an account
  • Student photos are only used with written parental consent
  • We never share student data with unauthorized third parties

Eastgate Academy will NEVER contact parents via WhatsApp or phone to request fee payments. All payments are made exclusively through this official portal. Report any suspicious contact to us immediately on 0244 512 123.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in during your session
  • Remember your dark mode preference
  • Store your cookie consent choice
  • Improve website performance

You can decline cookies using the banner on our website. Note that declining cookies may affect some portal features.

7. Payments & Security

All fee payments are processed through our secure portal. We accept MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash and AirtelTigo Money.

  • Every payment generates a unique reference number
  • Payment records are stored securely in our database
  • Digital receipts are available through the parent portal
  • We never store mobile money PINs or account credentials

Never send money to any personal mobile number claiming to be from Eastgate Academy. All official payments are made through this portal only.

8. Portal Use

By using the Eastgate Academy portal you agree to:

  • Keep your login credentials confidential
  • Not share your Student ID or password with others
  • Log out after each session especially on shared devices
  • Report any suspicious activity to the school immediately
  • Not attempt to access other users' accounts or data

Eastgate Academy reserves the right to suspend portal access for any user found to be misusing the system.

9. Your Rights

As a parent or guardian you have the right to:

  • Request access to all data we hold about your child
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data when your child leaves the school
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights please contact us using the details below.
