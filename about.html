About Us

Building a legacy of learning, leadership and growth since 2010.

Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Eastgate Academy has been a cornerstone of quality primary education in Dawenya, Tema. What began as a small neighbourhood school has grown into a respected institution serving families throughout the region.

Over the years we have built a reputation for academic excellence, character development and a genuine love of learning — values that guide everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, stimulating learning environment where children aged 5–12 can develop intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically through a balanced curriculum and dedicated teaching staff.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every interaction we have.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all areas of school life, academics and beyond.

Respect
